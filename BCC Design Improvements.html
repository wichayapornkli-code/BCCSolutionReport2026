Improvement 01

Search & product finding.

Finding

Users struggle to find products when the keyword is not typed exactly as expected — incomplete keywords, inconsistent spacing, abbreviations, or different wording all break results.

What to do

  • Add fuzzy search for non-exact keywords
  • Support variations such as “600V”, “600 V”, “600 volt”
  • Synonym matching for product names and specs
  • Suggest related products when no exact result is found
  • Clearer search placeholder and hint text

Improvement 02

All products & filtering.

Finding

Users need to browse the full product list and move across categories without returning to the homepage. A complex catalogue needs strong filtering — but the interface still has to feel easy to scan.

What to do

  • Add an All Products entry point
  • Switch categories in place, without going home
  • Grouped filters: product group, voltage, type, market
  • Applied filter chips · result count · clear reset
  • Keep filter structure scannable at scale

Improvement 03

Product detail & specifications.

Finding

Users need to access specifications quickly. Some information lives in PDFs, but key details should also be easier to find directly on the product page.

What to do

  • PDF download button near the spec table
  • Bring important PDF details into the page where possible
  • Improve spec table readability, especially on mobile
  • Shortcuts to Specification, Cable Weight, Download
  • Microcopy such as “Download full specification”

Improvement 04

Documents & certifications.

Finding

Users have difficulty identifying which file or certificate they need when cards lack clear titles, categories, or descriptions.

What to do

  • Clear titles on every download and certificate card
  • File category — Catalog, Certificate, Product Sheet
  • Metadata: file type, category, updated date
  • Short descriptions explaining what each file is
  • Search or filters by document type

Improvement 05

Navigation & information architecture.

Finding

Users need clearer paths for product discovery, specification lookup, certification access, downloads, and B2B distribution inquiries.

What to do

  • Restructure navigation around key user tasks
  • Clearer shortcuts to Products, Certifications, Downloads
  • Move easily between discovery, specs, and certifications
  • Clearer path for B2B distribution inquiries
  • Reduce guessing where content belongs

Improvement 06

Brand story & trust building.

Finding

The website feels modern, but credibility and brand history could be communicated more clearly. The corporate video can work harder as a trust-building asset.

What to do

  • Enlarge the corporate video area
  • Make the company story section more prominent
  • Clearer history, founding year, milestones
  • Factory, team, project, and quality visuals
  • Tighter spacing, typography, and hierarchy
